# Core concepts

Short definitions for the main building blocks.

- Account: holds cash, positions, trades, equity, and margin state.
- Cash asset: a currency balance registered in the account (e.g. USD, EUR).
- Instrument: trading contract metadata (symbols, settlement, margin, lifecycle).
- Order: intent to trade at a price and quantity.
- Trade: realized fill generated by `fill_order!`.
- Position: netted exposure per instrument, with average entry price.
- Settlement styles: Asset, Cash, VariationMargin (affects cashflows and equity).
- Margin modes: None, PercentNotional, FixedPerContract (affects required margin).
- Collectors: time-series samplers for balances, equity, drawdowns, etc.
